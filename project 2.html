REBRANDING


Project Brief

Zepto is your next-door quick commerce app, delivering online groceries, fruits, vegetables, personal care, electronics & much more to you in just minutes. Zepto's target audience is generally young adults and the modern generation. I have redesigned the logo for ZEPTO. The alphabet Z in the new logo represents the flash/lightening sign to highlight the usp of the brand (quick delivery) I have also given this brand a new identity by changing its color scheme. The new colors for this brand are navy blue(trust) , pink(positive energy, kind) , yellow(happiness) and white(stability).
