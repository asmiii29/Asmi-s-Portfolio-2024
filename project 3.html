PACKAGING DESIGN


Project Brief

I have designed a packaging box for a scented candle. The design is inspired by moon and space. The font in which the name of the candle is written is also designed by me. The font is inspired by the crescent phase of the moon. The packaging has a simple yet stricking design. The name of this hypothetical brand is moon itself, with alphabet 'M' as its logo. The colors used are simple yet meaningful. The front plane of the packaging has a circle cutout from which the inside moon-shaped spherical candle will be visible.
